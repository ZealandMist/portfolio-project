About Me

I caught the coding bug late in life. However, there was no going back after my first HTML, CSS, and JavaScript class.

Being a front-end developer allows me to take my theater and art education background to a new level. I’m still able to take a design and make it a reality – similar to taking a costume design and creating a garment for an actor to wear on stage. My main goals are always to make sure that user interfaces are simple and accessible. In addition, I focus on building features that solve key client problems in impactful ways.

These days you may find me nose down in code versus fabric and paint, but you can sure bet there is still a musical soundtrack playing in the background while I do it.
